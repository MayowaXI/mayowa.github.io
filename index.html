About me

I am an adaptable IT Technician with a robust foundation in electrical engineering. My passion lies in diagnosing and resolving hardware issues, setting up new systems, and ensuring seamless business operations. With a knack for quickly adapting to evolving situations, I consistently enhance technical workflows and support technological advancements. My experience spans troubleshooting, system configuration, and user support, enabling me to contribute effectively to any tech environment.

My technical skills include installing and configuring Windows 10, handling Active Directory tasks, and troubleshooting various system problems. I am proficient in Microsoft Office Suite and have a working knowledge of Windows Server 2016, PowerShell, Remote Access, and VMware. I also have hands-on experience with new feature testing, requirements analysis, and manual testing, which equips me to address diverse IT challenges with efficiency.

Previously, I worked as a Computer Science Teacher and IT Support Specialist, where I improved student performance and reduced downtime by swiftly resolving IT issues. My internships in electrical engineering provided me with valuable experience in design reviews and quality control. I hold a Graduate Certificate in Cloud Computing, a BSc in Electrical Engineering, and am CompTIA A+ certified. I am fluent in English and committed to continuous learning and professional growth.
